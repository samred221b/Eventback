# Eventopia – Vendor Overview

A clear, non-technical summary of Eventopia for partners, venues, and event organizers.

## What Is Eventopia?
Eventopia is a mobile app that helps people discover, save, and share events happening around them. It brings attendees and organizers together in one simple, modern experience.

## Who It’s For
- **Attendees**: People looking for concerts, conferences, nightlife, sports, workshops, and community happenings.
- **Organizers & Venues**: Event creators, brands, and spaces who want to publish events, reach the right audience, and build loyalty.

## What You Can Do
- **As an attendee**
  - Browse featured and upcoming events.
  - Search by name, category, city, or venue.
  - View rich event pages with key details (date, time, location, pricing, host).
  - Save favorites for quick access.
  - Share events with friends and get directions in one tap.

- **As an organizer**
  - Publish new events with clear descriptions, images, and pricing.
  - Manage your events from a simple dashboard.
  - See basic insights like views, likes, and top categories.

## Key Benefits
- **Reach the right audience**: Your events appear where people are actively looking for things to do.
- **Increase attendance**: Clear details, simple sharing, and quick directions remove friction from discovery to attendance.
- **Build loyalty**: Favorites and reminders help your audience come back.
- **Save time**: A straightforward flow to create and update events.

## Feature Highlights
- **Discovery**: Curated highlights for featured and upcoming events.
- **Search & Categories**: Quick filters by topic (music, culture, education, sports, business, etc.) and location.
- **Calendar View**: See what’s happening today, this week, and this month at a glance.
- **Event Pages**: Beautiful layout with schedule, venue, pricing, host, and a detailed description.
- **Share & Directions**: Spread the word and guide guests to your venue with a tap.
- **Favorites**: Attendees can save events and return to them later.
- **Organizer Tools**: Create events, manage listings, and review basic engagement insights.

## Screens at a Glance
- **Welcome**: Brand introduction and a simple “Get Started” action.
- **Home**: Quick discovery with highlights and easy search access.
- **Events**: Full catalog with categories, search, and sorting.
- **Calendar**: Date-first view to check what’s happening on specific days.
- **Event Details**: Complete information, share button, and directions to the venue.
- **Favorites**: Attendees’ saved events in one place.
- **Organizer Login**: Sign in to access organizer tools.
- **Organizer Dashboard**: Your events, their status (upcoming/today/ended), and basic insights.
- **Create Event**: A guided form to publish new events with images and key details.

## Typical Flows
- **Attendee journey**
  1. Open the app and explore featured/upcoming events.
  2. Search or filter by category or location.
  3. Open an event to view details, then share it or get directions.
  4. Save favorites to revisit later.

- **Organizer journey**
  1. Sign in as an organizer.
  2. Create a new event with date, time, location, and pricing.
  3. Add an image and a compelling description.
  4. Publish, then track basic interest through views and likes.

## Why Partner with Eventopia?
- **Discoverability**: Your events are placed where motivated audiences are browsing.
- **Professional presentation**: Clean design that highlights what matters most.
- **Faster conversions**: Clear details, sharing, and maps help turn interest into attendance.
- **Audience insight**: Simple indicators of interest help you understand what resonates.

## Next Steps
If you’re a venue, brand, or event organizer and want to showcase your events on Eventopia:
- Prepare your event details (title, description, date, time, location, pricing, and an image).
- Sign in as an organizer and publish your listing.
- Share your Eventopia links with your audience to maximize reach.
